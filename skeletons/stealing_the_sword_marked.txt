=== BEAT: 01_caught_in_the_act ===
so the cat is walking through the corridor and he hears the odd noises coming from the throne room. the cat comes in and there's a fox, by the throne. there's a the crown, askew on his head and the magic sword in his paws, sheathed. fox is pulling the sword out of the scabbard desperately, trying to draw the blade and it doesn't budge.
- I'd ask you what you are doing but...
- I'm stealing the sword
- I can see that - cat sighs
- What? It's a magic sword. Do you have any idea what it costs?
- No. I don't. Do you?
- Well, no. Not yet - fox is pulling hard but it's a no go.

=== BEAT: 02_the_firebird_arrives ===
Suddenly the Firebird and two guards appear behind the cat in the doorway. They freeze as they enter, taking the scene in.
- That's not what you think - fox drops the sword and gives a sly smile.
- It'd better not be. Stealing the magic sword is a capital crime. The punishment is death - Firebird's eyes start to glow amber.
- It's... I mean, I was just waking by and...
- I asked him to - cat, plainly.
- You did? - both fox and the firebird.
- But... why? - firebird.
- It... called to me. Yes. So I had to... investigate.
- The sword called to you? I see - firebird tilts her head slightly
- Yes, that's what happened - cat, then to fox - can we go now?
- Prove it - firebird's voice calm but firm.
- What?
- Prove it. Draw the blade.
- Is it really necessary? I mean...
- Draw it - firebird's voice grew hard. - or I'll fry you where you stand. Both of you. As the law commands.

=== BEAT: 03_drawing_the_blade ===
There's a pause. Guards exchange nervous glances. Cat sighs.
- Fine.
He walks slowly to where fox stands over the sword.
- Behind the pillar first, then into the window - fox whispers.
Cat takes the sword from the floor and puts his right paw on the handle.
- Get ready - whispers fox
There's a pause and then cat pulls the handle slightly. Soft click and blade slides out with no effort to the third of the length. There's a silence in the room. Everyone, fox, firebird, guards and cat himself stare at the blade, sun dancing on the dark polished metal. Nobody says anything. Nobody moves. Then cat slides the blade back and looks at the firebird.
- As I said...

=== BEAT: 04_custody_of_the_sword ===
fox grabs the sword from the cat
- We're taking it, thanks, bye.
fox is already walking to the exit when cat grabs him and puts his paw back on the sword. fox pulls the sword away, cat pulls it toward. they push and pull the sword for a few times then fox:
- Fine, you have it - he releases the sword.
It unbalances the cat and he almost topples over. Quickly cat recovers and stands up straight.
- What are you going to do with it? - asks the firebird.
- I was thinking... I was hoping to leave it in your care - says the cat.
fox gives him the look.
- If you don't mind - cat hands the sword over to the firebird. fox sighs.
- Thank you.
firebird takes the sword carefully and holds it by the scabbard.
- I'll make sure it's available. When needed.
Fox shakes his head in disbelieve and starts towards the exit still wearing the crown. cat follows. firebird eyes them closely and as they pass, she  takes the crown off the fox's head.
- I'll be keeping this safe as well.
- You're a monster, anyone told you that? - fox is crushed
firebird eyes grow cold.
- Screamed more likely. But yes.
she motions towards the exit, then to the guards:
- Make sure our... guests make it out... uneventful.

=== BEAT: 05_the_walk_out ===
cat and fox walk out of the throne room and walk slowly down the corridor, guards a few paces behind them. There's silence, then fox whispers:
- You drew the blade...
- I know, right
they both laugh, on and on as they walk and guards exchange confused glances behind them.
